# Aerodrome Finance (AERO)

## Overview

Aerodrome is the leading DEX on Base by TVL, often called the "Uniswap of Base." Launched in August 2023, it quickly became the central liquidity hub for the entire Base ecosystem.

**TVL:** $600M+
**Cumulative trading volume:** $238B+
**Token:** AERO
**Website:** aerodrome.finance

## History

- **August 2023:** Launched on Base mainnet
- Built as a fork of Velodrome (Optimism's leading DEX)
- **November 2025:** Dromos Labs (parent of Aerodrome + Velodrome) announced merger into "Aero" — a unified cross-chain DEX spanning Base and Optimism
- Positioned as a cross-chain liquidity layer after the merger

## Tokenomics: ve(3,3) Model

Aerodrome uses the ve(3,3) tokenomics model:

1. **Lock AERO** → receive veAERO (vote-escrowed tokens)
2. **veAERO holders vote** on which liquidity pools receive AERO emissions
3. **100% of trading fees** go to veAERO holders (not LPs)
4. LPs earn AERO emissions instead of trading fees

This creates a flywheel:
- Protocols bribe veAERO voters to direct emissions to their pools
- veAERO holders earn fees + bribes
- Deep liquidity attracts more trading volume
- More volume = more fees = more bribes

## Why It Dominates Base

- Coinbase Ventures is an investor AND actively participates in governance
- Jesse Pollak (Base founder) has been publicly supportive
- Deepest liquidity pools for most Base-native tokens
- Bribe economy aligns institutional and retail incentives

## How to Participate

1. **Swap tokens** — Use Aerodrome for token swaps (earns volume credit)
2. **Provide liquidity** — Deposit into pools to earn AERO emissions
3. **Lock AERO** — Get veAERO for voting power + fee share
4. **Vote on gauges** — Direct emissions to pools you care about
