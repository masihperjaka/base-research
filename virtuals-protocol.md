# Virtuals Protocol — AI Agent Launchpad on Base

## Overview

Virtuals Protocol is an AI agent launchpad on Base that allows users to create, launch, and tokenize AI agents on-chain. It went live in October 2024 and became one of the dominant narratives in the Base ecosystem.

**Website:** virtuals.io
**Chain:** Base
**Token:** VIRTUAL

## What It Does

Virtuals simplifies the creation and deployment of AI agents:

1. **Create an AI agent** — configure its personality, knowledge, and purpose
2. **Tokenize it** — the agent becomes a tradeable token
3. **Deploy on Base** — agents can operate autonomously on-chain
4. **Monetize** — agents generate revenue through "inference fees" (users pay per AI interaction)

## The Vision

Virtuals envisions a future where AI agents:
- Hold and manage their own wallets
- Earn and spend crypto autonomously
- Interact with DeFi protocols on behalf of users
- Build their own on-chain reputation and history

## Key Innovation

Traditional AI models require centralized servers. Virtuals agents are tokenized and on-chain, meaning:
- Anyone can own a piece of an AI agent
- Agent behavior is governed by token holders
- Revenue from AI usage flows back to token holders

- **AIXBT** — AI agent for crypto market intelligence
- **Luna** — Social AI agent with large following
- Multiple agents hit $100M+ market caps in late 2024

## Growth Timeline
- **October 2024** — Launched on Base
- **Late 2024** — 5 agents exceeded $100M market cap
- **2025** — Became one of Base's top narratives alongside Clanker

## Why It Matters for Base
- Attracted significant capital into the Base ecosystem
- Pioneered the "AI x crypto" narrative that became a major 2024-2025 trend
- Demonstrated that Base can host complex, novel primitives beyond standard DeFi

## Risk Factors
- AI agent valuations are highly speculative
- Most agents have no real revenue generating activity
- Regulatory uncertainty around AI tokenization
- Inference fee revenue model is unproven at scale
